13 / THE ENGINE · WHY WE SEE WHAT THE WALKTHROUGH MISSES

A retail consultant walks a store once and gives you an opinion. We've built something different — a shopper-physics simulator. Twenty hand-tuned Tier-2/3 family personas. A* pathfinding over your real floor plan. Per-fixture salience scoring with raycast occlusion to detect what's actually visible from where.

We don't predict what's already happened. We predict what happens if you move fixture X next to fixture Y. Every layout, 1,000 trips, overnight. The output is a ranked playbook of moves, each with predicted lift and a confidence interval.
