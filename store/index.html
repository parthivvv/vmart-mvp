Section 03 · Where the revenue went

Billing queue compresses through the evening peak.

Under baseline, Counter 2 only opens when the queue hits 15 (and Counter 3 at 20) — by which point shoppers in the queue have already crossed their tolerance thresholds. Optimized v2 schedules Counter 2 at 15:00, Counter 3 at 16:00, and Counter 4 at 17:30 — well ahead of the evening rush. The collapsing-wait gap is where the recovered revenue lives.

Section 04 · Trial-room throughput

Trial-room queue clears under attended-room enforcement.

T01 is reassigned from "floor relief" to the women's trial bank. The 4-items-per-cubicle limit is now enforced. Cubicle turnover drops ~25% — and the queue length collapses with it, because the tail of the distribution (10-min+ waits) was driving most of the trial-room abandonments.

Where the lift comes from.

Three operating levers are wired in this engine. Each shifts a specific environment variable — never agent behaviour or RNG. The causal chain is auditable: policy change → environment change → agent reacts → revenue moves.
